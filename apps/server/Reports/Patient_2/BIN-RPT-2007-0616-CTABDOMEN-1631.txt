CT OF THE ABDOMEN AND PELVIS

** HISTORY **:
Left upper quadrant pain, nausea, vomiting, leukocytosis.  Rule
out abscess, tumor

** FINDINGS **:
CT of the abdomen and pelvis was done following oral and during
intravenous contrast infusion.

There are a few scattered diverticula in the colon but no findings
that suggest acute diverticulitis.  The gallbladder and bile ducts
appear normal.

There is a 1.2 cm spherical cortical mass in the right mid kidney
posteriorly.  This measures between 100 and 130 Hounsfield units
and therefore appears to be a solid mass although it may be a
hyperdense cyst.  Further evaluation is recommended.  Consider
ultrasound and CT following day renal mass protocol for further
assessment.  There are no findings that suggest acute aisle
arthritis.

No evidence of bowel obstruction or splenic pathology.  No
findings to suggest pancreatitis.
