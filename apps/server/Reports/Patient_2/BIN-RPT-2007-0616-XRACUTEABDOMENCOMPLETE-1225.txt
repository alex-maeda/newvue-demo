ACUTE ABDOMEN

** HISTORY **:
Abdominal pain.

** FINDINGS **:
Two views of the abdomen as well as upright view of the chest
shows a relative paucity of bowel gas in the abdomen.  There is no
evidence of mechanical obstruction or visceral perforation.  Air
is seen within the rectal region.  There is no evidence of free
air beneath the hemidiaphragms.  The heart and lungs are normal.

** IMPRESSION **:
